## 🥇 Concept 1 — "Hot Seat" · debate flashpoints  *(THE AGITATION BET)*

- **Thesis / editorial identity:** the single most polarizing 20–40s exchange from structured
  debate shows, framed to force the viewer to pick a side and argue it in the comments. The
  channel's identity is *"the moment everyone's arguing about, isolated."*
- **Why it wins:** debate tops the live velocity chart (Jubilee 51.7K/hr). Comments + shares
  are the highest-weighted early signals, and debate-bait manufactures both — a debate clip
  outruns a higher-view non-debate clip. Structured debate has a YouTube hate-policy carve-out,
  and the **Jan 2026 update** *expanded* monetization on non-graphic controversial topics.
- **Feeders:** Jubilee · No Jumper · Pop Culture Crisis · Modern Day Debate · PBD (debate
  moments) · Flagrant (controversy) · the Whatever clip channels.
- **Format + hook:** cold-open *inside* the clash (never the calm intro); the polarizing claim
  as bold on-screen text in frame 1 (lands on mute); cut to reaction faces; **end on the
  unresolved question + "Who's right? 👇"** so the fight happens in the comments. Reply to 50+
  comments in the first 2 hrs (≈15–20% more reach).
- **Lane:** owned (YPP asset). High velocity, builds an audience fast.
- **⛔ Hard policy guardrail:** attack the **position/behavior**, never the **protected
  group/person**. "Splitting the bill signals you don't want equality" = SAFE. "[Group] are
  inferior/a disease" = STRUCK. **Hard-exclude the Fresh & Fit grievance lane** (same velocity,
  opposite monetization outcome). Treat trans-targeted content as RED regardless of ambiguity.
- **Go/No-Go:** universal test, **plus** track comment-rate — greenlight also if comment-rate
  >2× your channel baseline even at modest views (it's the leading indicator here).

## 🥈 Concept 2 — "Money Fights" · finance conflict  *(highest-EV open lane)*

- **Thesis:** real emotional money conflict + net-worth reveals + "this business prints $X/mo."
  Identity: *"the money conversations people don't say out loud."*
- **Why it wins:** finance is the highest US owned-channel RPM, and the conflict/confession
  angle is **under-clipped**. Ramit's "Money for Couples" (real couples fighting over money) is
  the single best open lane found this cycle — elite emotional fuel, advertiser-safe.
- **Feeders:** Ramit Sethi (lead) · Iced Coffee Hour · Graham Stephan · My First Million ·
  Codie Sanchez · Mark Cuban/Trailblazers.
- **Format + hook:** lead with the dollar number or the fight ("They make $400K and can't stop
  fighting about $40"); caption the tension; pay off the reveal at the end.
- **Lane:** owned (high-RPM YPP).
- **Go/No-Go:** universal test.

## 🥉 Concept 3 — "Crash Out" · comedy & reaction  *(volume → YPP)*

- **Thesis:** self-contained comedic/roast/rage beats that need zero context. Identity: *"the
  bit you'll text your group chat."*
- **Why it wins:** comedy travels widest (highest raw view ceiling), feeding owned-channel
  growth toward the YPP threshold. Lead with the **open lanes**: Bad Friends + This Is Important
  (no official clips channel = white space).
- **Feeders:** Bad Friends · Kill Tony · Flagrant · This Is Important · (Theo Von — saturated,
  use sparingly).
- **Format + hook:** punchline-first, fast cuts, no setup. Caption the funniest line.
- **Lane:** owned (YPP volume). ⚠ Test Content ID on This Is Important (iHeart) before scaling.
- **Go/No-Go:** universal test, but raise the bar — comedy median ≥10K **OR** ≥1 clip >250K
  (it should travel further or it's not landing).

## Concept 4 — "Myth-Busting" · health & fitness  *(evergreen, advertiser-safe)*

- **Thesis:** counterintuitive, screenshot-bait expert takes — *"research actually says X."*
  Identity: *"the 30-second correction to what you believed."*
- **Why it wins:** evergreen back-catalog (low time-pressure), sticky and shareable, and
  advertiser-safe **if** you clip personas/positions, not prescriptions.
- **Feeders:** Dr Mike Israetel (encourages clips) · Bryan Johnson (viral ceiling) · Jeff
  Nippard · Dr Gabrielle Lyon (whitespace) · Dr Rhonda Patrick · Layne Norton.
- **Format + hook:** "You've been told X. You're wrong." → the correction → the receipt.
- **Lane:** owned. ⛔ Avoid fringe supplement/medical claims (demonetization), Huberman (terms),
  Attia (paywall). Clip the *myth-bust*, never a health prescription.
- **Go/No-Go:** universal test.
